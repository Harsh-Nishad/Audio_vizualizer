what does our product provides?

Visualization has a great potential to increase your physical coordination at the instrument and improve speed. It can also greatly accelerate the speed at which you memorize a piece.

Audio visualizer aims to build a vibrant environment for the audience that enjoys a live concert of their favourite artist, by providing a live synced visual for every beat of the song played at the concert.
